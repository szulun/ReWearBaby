From Peru, With Love

Our founder is from Peru, bringing authentic connections to one of the world's finest textile traditions.

When baby clothes reach the end of their wearable life, we don't throw them away. Instead, they travel back to Peru, where skilled artisans transform them into beautiful new garments using traditional weaving techniques.

This creates stable employment for Peruvian families while preserving centuries-old craftsmanship.
